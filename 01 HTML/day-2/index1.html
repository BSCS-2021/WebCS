My Favorite Hidden Gems in Lahore:

Lahore Fort:
This magnificent fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and features stunning architecture, beautiful gardens, and historical artifacts.
Wazir Khan Mosque:
This 17th-century mosque is known for its intricate tile work and vibrant colors. It's a must-see for anyone interested in Islamic art and architecture.
Badshahi Mosque:
This mosque is one of the largest in the world and is a great example of Mughal architecture. It's particularly beautiful at sunset.
Lahore Food Street:
This is a narrow alley full of small restaurants and food vendors offering traditional Pakistani cuisine. It's a great place to try local specialties like biryani, kebabs, and haleem.

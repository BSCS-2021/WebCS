About Pakistan

Pakistan, a country in South Asia, is known for its diverse geography, rich culture, and warm hospitality. From the towering peaks of the Karakoram range to the fertile plains of Punjab, Pakistan offers a wide range of experiences for travelers.

Immerse yourself in the historical sites of Lahore, trek through the stunning valleys of Gilgit-Baltistan, or indulge in the vibrant street food scene of Karachi. Pakistan's breathtaking landscapes, ancient ruins, and vibrant festivals are sure to leave a lasting impression.

Lahore is the capital of The Punjab and is the country's 2nd largest city after Karachi.
